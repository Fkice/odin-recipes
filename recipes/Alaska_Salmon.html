Alaska Salmon Bake with Pecan Crunch Coating

Baked salmon makes an excellent main course!

Description

The Alaska Salmon Bake with Pecan Crunch Coating recipe is a delicious dish that combines the tenderness of salmon with a crunchy pecan coating. Below are the key elements of this preparation:

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 5 teaspoons honey
  • ½ cup fresh bread crumbs
  • ½ cup finely chopped pecans
  • 3 teaspoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 6 (4 ounce) fillets salmon
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • lemon wedges

Directions

  1. Step 1 Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). In a small bowl, mix together the mustard, butter, and honey. In another bowl, mix together the bread crumbs, pecans, and parsley.
  2. Step 2Season each salmon fillet with salt and pepper. Place on a lightly greased baking sheet. Brush with mustard-honey mixture. Cover the top of each fillet with bread crumb mixture.
  3. Step 3Bake for 10 minutes per inch of thickness, measured at thickest part, or until salmon just flakes when tested with a fork. Serve garnished with lemon wedges
